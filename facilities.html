Facilities

1. Modern Library

Our library boasts a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources to aid in research and learning.

Professional counselors available to guide students in making informed career choices and planning their future.

Encouraging students to participate in community service and social outreach programs for holistic development.

Various clubs and societies focusing on arts, culture, music, and more to promote extracurricular interests.

Creative art studios with tools and materials for students interested in fine arts and design.
